About us

Here's our story!

As a teenage Indian student, you’re often told to study now- and that will lead you to all the opportunities you’ll want and need in the future. But what if by spending all your school time on studying, you’re missing out on the biggest opportunity possible? At the dropout, we aim to cover that. We cover all the methods and tools a student or a dropout can use to be productive, and make money, no schooling needed. Only perseverance and common sense. Covering topics ranging from the stock market, niche websites, and full-fledged start ups. Through our journey in this fields, we hope to give you all an idea of how these things work, and what you can do to make some cash and have fun while doing it.
